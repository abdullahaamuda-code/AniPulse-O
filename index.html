How to Use AniPulse

1. Type the anime name in the search box and press Enter.
2. All available seasons (according to the database) will be displayed.
3. Click an anime card to see detailed info: episodes, status, next airing, and images.
4. Note: Some anime may only show one season if future seasons aren't listed in the API.
5. Enjoy exploring your favorite anime!
